LSXMPlayer 1.0 by Alurcard2

This module will let you play extended module (.xm) files.

Settings:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 XMPlayerVolume [0 through 25, default=25]
 - Sets the volume to play XM files at.

Bangs
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
!PlayXM [file]
 - Plays the XM file.
!PlayXMNoLoop [file]
 - An XM track plays repeatedly by default. Use this bang to play it only once.
!PauseXM
 - Pause any currently playing XM file.
!ResumeXM
 - Resumes any paused XM file.
!SetXMVolume [0 through 25]
 - Sets the volume to play XM files at.
!StopXM
 - Stops any currently playing XM file.
